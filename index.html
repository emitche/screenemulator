About Screen Emulator

You may be asking, "What's a screen emulator?" or "Why does this matter?".

If you've ever designed responsively, testing different browsers and screen sizes is incredibly important. I wanted to provide a simple, free tool to test sites and apps. Sometimes designing and developing responsively involves considering screen sizes and pixel counts that you may not have access to with your current screen size. This tool lets you specify dimensions and view what that site looks like at the size or resolution.
